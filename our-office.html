The Web Plant / Our Office

STEP INSIDE AND TAKE A SEAT

If you hadn’t already figured it out, we’re The Webplant – a software and service company based in New Delhi, India.

Founded in 2011, we’ve grown rapidly in just a couple of years with an amazing, dynamic team. We love strong coffee, the latest technology, strange internet memes, and accomplishing great things through better, stronger relationships. We noticed that business software is often something people are forced to use, not enjoy using. We knew that needed to change.

We have grown from a single room to a 10,000 sq ft office.

We have grown from 2 employees to 150 employees.

We have grown from small number of projects to more than 20,000 projects.

The end result is the The Webplant you see today; a growing, learning organism that’s constantly seeking to be better — for our customers, our product, our team, and our investors.
